Childcare Near Changi — Getting to Genius Gems

Our centre sits at 26 Mariam Close, Singapore 508668, a quiet close off Old Tampines Road in the Changi area — minutes from Pasir Ris, Tampines, and Loyang, and an easy drive for families across the East.

A Neighbourhood Made for Little Explorers

Genius Gems is a bilingual childcare centre and preschool serving families from Changi, Pasir Ris, Tampines, and Bedok. Being tucked into a quiet close means calm drop-offs away from busy main-road traffic, room for our large outdoor playground, and a peaceful setting where children can learn and play — while parents remain minutes from the major roads that carry them on to work.

Getting Here by MRT

The nearest MRT station is Pasir Ris (EW1) on the East–West Line, approximately 3.5 km from the centre. From the station, it is a short hop by bus or car to Mariam Close.

Getting Here by Bus

Several bus stops sit within walking distance of the centre:

  • Aft Jln Mariam (97109) — service 29
  • Bef Jln Mariam (97101) — service 29
  • Aft Toh Cl (97119) — services 5 and 29
  • Opp Changi Women's Prison (97051) — services 2 and 5
  • Changi Women's Prison (97059) — services 2 and 5

Driving Directions & Drop-Off

From Loyang Avenue (heading from Changi towards Tampines):

  1. Turn left into Old Tampines Road
  2. Turn right into Mariam Way (near Mariam Way Playground)
  3. Turn left into Mariam Close
  4. The centre is about 50 m on the left

Street parking is available nearby. We are open 7:00am–7:00pm on weekdays and 7:00am–1:00pm on Saturdays, so drop-off and pick-up can flex around your working hours — contact us and we will help you plan a routine that works for your family.
