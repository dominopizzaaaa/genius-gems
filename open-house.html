The Experience

What to Expect on the Day

A warm, hands-on morning designed for the whole family — explore at your own pace, ask us anything, and let your little one dive right in.

Guided Campus Tour

Walk through our bright, purpose-built classrooms, cosy reading corners, and outdoor play spaces.

Curriculum Showcase

Discover our bilingual, play-based programme and how we nurture curiosity, confidence and kindness.

Hands-on Activity Stations

Sensory play, art & craft, a Mandarin corner and STEM discovery — your child can try it all.

Meet Our Teachers

Chat with our warm, qualified educators and hear how we care for every child's unique brilliance.

Music, Movement & Stories

Sing-alongs, storytelling and mini music sessions that show learning at its most joyful.

Refreshments & Mingling

Enjoy light bites while you chat with our team and other families over a relaxed morning.

Programme

Open House Schedule

The same joyful programme runs on both Saturdays — 8 & 15 August 2026. Drop in anytime; there's something happening all morning through to mid-afternoon.

  1. 9:00am

    Welcome & Registration

    Sign in at our front desk, pick up your family activity map, and enjoy a warm welcome (and refreshments!).

  2. 9:30am

    Principal's Welcome & Curriculum Talk

    A short, friendly overview of the Genius Gems philosophy and our bilingual, play-based approach.

  3. 10:00am

    Guided Campus Tours Begin

    Small-group walk-throughs of our classrooms, discovery zones and outdoor play areas.

  4. 10:45am

    Hands-on Learning Stations

    Sensory play, art & craft, the Mandarin corner and a STEM discovery table open for little explorers.

  5. 11:30am

    Music, Movement & Storytime

    Interactive sing-alongs and storytelling that bring our classroom magic to life.

  6. 12:15pm

    Mini Sports & Outdoor Play

    Fun, active games in our outdoor space — bring comfy clothes for the little ones!

  7. 1:00pm

    Light Lunch & Family Mingling

    Relax over light refreshments and get to know our team and community.

  8. 1:45pm

    Meet-the-Teachers & Q&A

    One-to-one time with our educators to answer your questions about your child's journey.

  9. 2:30pm

    Enrolment Desk & Open House Offers

    Ready to join us? Speak to our team about places and exclusive open-house enrolment perks.

  10. 3:00pm

    Open House Closes

    Thank you for visiting — we can't wait to welcome your family to Genius Gems!

Times are a guide — feel free to drop in whenever suits your family. Activity stations run throughout the morning.

Good to Know

Before You Come

Who's it for?

Families with children aged 18 months to 6 years. Curious siblings and grandparents are welcome too!

How much?

Completely free. Registering ahead simply helps us prepare enough goodie bags and refreshments.

What to wear

Comfy clothes and covered shoes for the little ones — there'll be plenty of hands-on play.

Getting here

Street parking is available nearby. We're a short ride from Changi and easily reached by bus.

How long to stay

Stay for as little as 30 minutes or the whole morning — it's entirely up to you.

Take-home treats

Every visiting family leaves with a little Genius Gems goodie bag and our curriculum guide.

Questions

Open House FAQ

Do I need to register in advance?

Walk-ins are always welcome, but we'd love you to RSVP so we can prepare enough refreshments and goodie bags, and reserve a guided tour slot for your family.

Can I bring my child along?

Absolutely — the Open House is designed for children! There are plenty of hands-on stations and activities for them to enjoy while you explore.

What ages is the Open House suitable for?

Our programmes cater to children from 18 months to 6 years. Families considering any of these age groups will find something for them.

How long should we plan to stay?

Come for 30 minutes or the whole morning — it's completely flexible. Activity stations run throughout, so there's always something happening.

Is there a cost to attend?

No — admission is completely free, and every visiting family goes home with a little Genius Gems goodie bag.

Will I be able to enrol on the day?

Yes! Our enrolment desk will be open, and there are special open-house perks for families who decide to join us.
