Why Genius Gems

What Makes Us Special

The activities run as drop-in booths throughout the day — there's no fixed timetable, so come whenever suits your family and take it all in at your own pace. Here's what you'll discover.

Learning Through Play

Every skill starts as a game. From balance trails to sensory stations, our little ones build confidence, coordination and curiosity without ever realising they're learning.

Strong Curriculum

Our bilingual programme is built so children step into Primary or Elementary School ready — confident readers, thinkers and question-askers.

Large Outdoor Playground

Real grass, big slides and room to run. Our outdoor space gives children the daily fresh-air play that growing bodies and busy minds need.

Lots of Family Involvement

Showcases, celebrations and workshops throughout the year — because the proudest moments are the ones you get to share with your child.

Free Enrichment, Included in Fees

Music, Computer, and Speech & Drama — all part of the programme, at no extra cost.

More Than 4 Excursions a Year

Real-world adventures beyond the classroom that children talk about for weeks.

Good to Know

Before You Come

Who's it for?

Families with children aged 18 months to 6 years. Curious siblings and grandparents are welcome too!

How much?

Admission is completely free — and sign up on the day for up to $800 worth of discounts.

What to wear

Comfy clothes and covered shoes for the little ones — there'll be plenty of hands-on play.

Getting here

Street parking is available nearby. We're a short ride from Changi and easily reached by bus.

How long to stay

Stay for 30 minutes or the whole day — booths run concurrently, so there's no fixed timetable to keep up with.

Take-home treats

Every visiting family leaves with a little Genius Gems goodie bag and our curriculum guide.

Questions

Open House FAQ

Do I need to register in advance?

Walk-ins are always welcome, but we'd love you to RSVP so we can prepare enough refreshments and goodie bags for your family.

Can I bring my child along?

Absolutely — the Open House is designed for children! There are plenty of hands-on stations and activities for them to enjoy while you explore.

What ages is the Open House suitable for?

Our programmes cater to children from 18 months to 6 years. Families considering any of these age groups will find something for them.

How long should we plan to stay?

Come for 30 minutes or the whole day — it's completely flexible. The booths all run concurrently with no fixed schedule, so you'll never miss anything by arriving later.

Is there a cost to attend?

No — admission is completely free, and every visiting family goes home with a little Genius Gems goodie bag.

Will I be able to enrol on the day?

Yes! Our enrolment desk will be open, and families who sign up on the day enjoy up to $800 worth of discounts.
