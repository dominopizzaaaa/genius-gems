Why Genius Gems

What Makes Us Special

For over 15 years our approach has stayed the same: children learn best through play — with a bilingual programme, free enrichment, real outdoor space and families welcomed in at every turn.

01

Learning Through Play

Every skill starts as a game. From balance trails to sensory stations, our little ones build confidence, coordination and curiosity without ever realising they're learning.

02

Strong Curriculum

Our bilingual programme is built so children step into Primary or Elementary School ready — confident readers, thinkers and question-askers. Small classes mean every child is truly known.

03

Large Outdoor Playground

Real grass, big slides and room to run. Our outdoor space gives children the daily fresh-air play that growing bodies and busy minds need — plus more than 4 excursions a year.

04

Lots of Family Involvement

Showcases, celebrations and workshops throughout the year — because the proudest moments are the ones you get to share with your child.

Good to Know

Before You Come

Who's it for?

Families with children aged 18 months to 6 years. Curious siblings and grandparents are welcome too!

How much?

Admission is completely free — and sign up on the day for up to $800 worth of discounts.

What to wear

Comfy clothes and covered shoes for the little ones — there'll be plenty of hands-on play.

Getting here

Street parking is available nearby. We're a short ride from Changi and easily reached by bus.

How long to stay

Stay for 30 minutes or the whole day — booths run concurrently, so there's no fixed timetable to keep up with.

How to book

Send us a WhatsApp with your preferred date — it's the quickest way to secure your slot.

Questions

Open House FAQ

Do I need to register in advance?

Walk-ins are always welcome, but we'd love you to WhatsApp us first so we can plan and prepare better for your family.

How do I book my Open House visit?

Simply WhatsApp us at +65 9655 3213 and let us know which Saturday suits you. We'll confirm your slot right away.

Can I bring my child along?

Absolutely — the Open House is designed for children! There are plenty of hands-on booths and activities for them to enjoy while you explore.

Is there a fixed schedule for the day?

No — the activities run as concurrent booths from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. There's no time order to keep up with, so you'll never miss anything by arriving later.

What ages is the Open House suitable for?

Our programmes cater to children from 18 months to 6 years. Families considering any of these age groups will find something for them.

Is the Open House free?

Yes — admission is completely free for all families.

Will I be able to enrol on the day?

Yes! Our enrolment desk will be open, and families who sign up on the day enjoy up to $800 worth of discounts. Terms and conditions apply.

What makes Genius Gems different?

Small class sizes, a fully bilingual programme, free enrichment included in fees (Music, Computer, Speech & Drama), a large outdoor playground, more than 4 excursions a year, and a genuine learning-through-play philosophy.
